For bad debts write off adjusting entry:
DR Allowance for Doubtful Debts dr - / cr +
	CR Accounts Receivable  dr + / cr -


- reduce the allowance (doubtful debts)
- reduce accounts receivable (asset)


Ex.
Write off $500 from a customer:
Dr Allowance for Doubtful Debts 500
	Cr Accounts Receivable 500
