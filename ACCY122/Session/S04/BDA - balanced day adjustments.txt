Session 4 is about adjusting entries

5 core adjustments:
1. Accrued Income
2. Prepaid Expenses
3. Unearned Revenue
4. Depreciation
5. Bad debt


1. Accrued Income = Earned but NOT received
You provided services worth $1K, but customer hasn’t paid yet.
- You earned it → income
- But no cash yet → someone owes you

Adjusting entry:
Dr Accounts Receivable (AR)  1,000  
   Cr Service Revenue          1,000  


2. Prepaid Expenses = Paid but NOT used yet
You paid $1.2K insurance for 12 months.
At year-end, only 3 months used ($300) → 9 months unused = $900

Adjusting entry:
Dr Insurance Expense   300  
   Cr Prepaid Insurance   300  


3. Unearned Revenue = Received but NOT earned (Sess 3 wkbk Qn4.12 -no4)
You received $4,8K rent for 4 months.
1 month earned ($1.2K) → 3 months unearned = $3.6K

Adjusting entry:
Dr Unearned Revenue   1,200  
   Cr Rental Revenue      1,200  


4. Depreciation = Asset losing value over time
Equipment cost = 12,000
Residual = 2,000 (resale value after useful life)
Life = 10 years

Annual depreciation = 1,000

Adjusting entry
Dr Depreciation Expense - equipment 1,000  
   Cr Accumulated Depreciation - equipment   1,000
