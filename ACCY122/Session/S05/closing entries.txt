Closing Entries

- closing the books for last year books 
- balance brought forward to new year 

Ex. 
31 dec 25 - close Entries (bal c/d)
1 jan 26 - open Entries (bal b/d)
31 dec 26 - close Entries (bal c/d)


there are always 4 closing Entries
- revenue reset to 0
- expense reset to 0
- transfer profit/loss to capital -> updates owner’s capital
- close drawings -> reduces capital


note:
The Profit or Loss Summary account is a temporary account used at 
the end of an accounting period to close revenue and expense accounts
- Profit → Credit balance in P&L Summary
- Loss → Debit balance in P&L Summary
