Accounting Cycle Order:
Record -> Sort -> Check -> Report

1. Journal 
2. Ledger 
3. Trial Balance 
4. Financial Statements



1. Journal
First step
Write down every transaction

2. Ledger (sort)
Group transactions into respective accounts (bank a/c, nca a/c, exp a/c, capital a/c, svc revenue a/c.)

3. Trial Bal (check)
Add everyth up and tally 
make sure debit = credit (amount has to tally)  

4. Financial Statements (report)
Now use the trial bal to prepare 
- income statement (profit or loss) -> P/L = TR - TE
- statement of OE -> OE at start + changes = OE at end
- bal sheet -> show asset, liabilities, equity  
- statement of cashflows -> cash movement
